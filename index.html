Project: Empty

Status: Paused

Project Empty is focused on easily making near perfect vacuums, free of almost all matter.

The current plan is to design a handheld tool that selects an area and moves all matter in that area outside.

The area would remain empty until the tool was toggles again.

Research has been on and off, only working on it as a sort of side project when everything else is done.

Project: Glide

Status: Archived

Notice: Project Glide has been put on hold indefinitly, and will not have any further work done on it.

Project Glide was focused on making a minimal friction surface, designed to almost not affect motion on it at all.

It was a primary project here at Parallel Labratories, and similar projects had been worked on at other companies.

Much like other companies, it was deemed too difficult to make a frictionless medium relative to rewards from it, and was discontinued.
